  • International Designs

Since there were few if any contemporary designs in India, international service apartment design elements like an emphasis on space, noise reduction, family spaces, self-contained living (kitchens), separate entrances as well as a low maintenance design.

  • Infrastructure Management

The local infrastructure in Gurgaon, Delhi, and Goa in terms of dependable electricity, water, safety, and connectivity are constantly improving. Taking into account the local infrastructure, our serviced apartments and guest houses were designed with electricity backup, water purification plants as well as fool proof security like CCTV camera, physical guards, high walls, fire extinguishers, and elevators as well as wide staircases.

  • Trained Staff & Employee Development

As service apartments have not yet evolved as a separate segment, our staff is mostly from the conventional hospitality background. We believe in Employee Development through a range of informal training and job enhancements. Our focus on training has helped improve both customer and employee satisfaction.

  • Multiple Property Types

Perch has recently added studio apartments in Gurgaon to its portfolio of operations. Studio apartment design and management are distinct from other shorter stay apartments/guest houses as it is a self-contained unit with lower servicing requirements.
